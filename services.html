Inovation In Pig Management

Innovation in pig managent around the world involves much more than pig houses. Farmers from small to large scale need to embrace digital solutions.

Pig House Management

We need to move from analogue to digital solutions. Smartphones and mobile devices such as tablets or desktop PCs are revolutionizing pig houses. Technological advances are making tasks secure, faster and easier.

Sow Management

Intelligent solution designs and equipment play an important role in sow management. The farmers economic success and the well being of sows and piglets are interconnected, each complimenting the other.

Piglet Rearing

Farmers producing piglets for the market have high expectations in regards to equipment they use. We at Jokama Farms exclusively endeavor to meet this expectation. The sensitive nature of piglets require specific high quality feed and responsible all round hygiene, much heat and protection from possible crushing by their mothers.

Jokama Farms AI Centre

Jokama Farms is establishing an Artificial Insemination Centre with a long term mission of being experts in freezing and thawing boar semen . A rigorous process using latest technology to select top 3% Boars for the AI station is undertaken. We look forward to giving our customers access to all the traditional breeds, as well as being able to Ship Fresh AI on a next day delivery around the country, they also come with Catheters to Inseminate gilts/sow.
